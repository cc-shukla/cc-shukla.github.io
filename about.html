Our Story

Founded in 2015, Ranjana Computer Institute began as a small computer repair shop with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable technology solutions to our community.

Over the years, we've grown from a single-person operation to a comprehensive technology center serving thousands of customers. Our commitment to excellence, continuous learning, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for computer services in the region.

Today, we offer a complete range of services including computer repair, education, IT consulting, and technical support. Our team of certified professionals brings decades of combined experience to every project.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and businesses with reliable technology solutions, expert education, and exceptional service that drives success and innovation.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology center in the region, recognized for excellence in service, innovation in education, and commitment to customer success.
